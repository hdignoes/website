Hugo Dignoes Ricart

PhD Student in Chemical Engineering

University of British Columbia  ·  Vancouver, BC, Canada

I am a PhD student in Chemical Engineering at the University of British Columbia. My MEng thesis focused on novel catalytic dehydrogenation processes for propylene production, and I continue that research thread as part of my doctoral work. I completed both my MEng and BASc at UBC — they just can't get rid of me! I majored in chemical process engineering and minored in analytical chemistry, with a particular interest in systems modelling and the human side of engineering.

In my free time I enjoy skiing, gardening, and small personal projects — like building a chess bot or setting up small-scale vertical farms in my basement.
