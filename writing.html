Natural Gas in a Sustainable World

The case for methane as part of a transition to clean energy

Natural gas and natural gas infrastructure can play a key role in a country's transition to clean energy, and might actually be the key to centralized renewable power storage. I make the case for the Sabatier process as a bridge technology — storing solar and wind energy as carbon-neutral methane using existing pipeline infrastructure.
